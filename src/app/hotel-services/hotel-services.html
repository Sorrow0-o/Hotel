Service 01

Beverages Included

From morning espresso to evening aperitivo — premium drinks flow freely throughout your stay.

Every AirGlo guest enjoys complimentary access to our full beverage programme. Begin your morning with a hand-poured single-origin coffee or a freshly squeezed juice from our breakfast bar. Throughout the day, our in-room minibar is replenished with still and sparkling water, artisan sodas, and seasonal infusions.

As evening falls, our lounge offers a curated selection of wines, craft cocktails, and premium spirits — all at no extra charge. Our sommeliers are on hand nightly to guide you through our cellar, which spans over 300 labels from Georgia, France, and Italy.

  • Complimentary in-room minibar refreshed daily
  • Specialty coffee & tea station open 06:00–22:00
  • Evening wine & cocktail hour in the lounge
  • 300+ label cellar with nightly sommelier
Service 02

Stay First, Pay Later

Reserve your room today and settle the bill on departure. No card holds, no surprises.

We believe your focus should be entirely on the experience, not the transaction. AirGlo's flexible payment model lets you book with confidence — no upfront charge, no deposit, and no credit card hold on arrival. Simply check in, enjoy every moment, and settle your account at checkout.

Our transparent billing means every item is itemised clearly. You'll receive a digital summary before checkout so there are never any surprises. For corporate guests, we also offer direct invoicing and deferred settlement arrangements.

  • No deposit or card hold on arrival
  • Itemised digital bill sent before checkout
  • All major cards, bank transfer & crypto accepted
  • Corporate direct invoicing available
Service 03

24 Hour Restaurant

Midnight cravings or early risers — our kitchen never sleeps. World-class cuisine, any hour.

AirGlo's signature restaurant operates around the clock, serving a rotating menu crafted by our Michelin-trained executive chef. Whether you arrive at 2am after a long flight or rise before sunrise for a morning ritual, our full kitchen is at your service — no compromise on quality, regardless of the hour.

Daytime sees a vibrant à la carte menu celebrating Georgian produce and Mediterranean influence. After midnight, our late-night kitchen offers comfort classics and lighter fare. In-room dining mirrors the restaurant menu exactly, delivered within 20 minutes.

  • Full kitchen service 24 hours, 7 days a week
  • Michelin-trained executive chef
  • In-room dining with 20-minute delivery
  • Seasonal Georgian & Mediterranean menu
Service 04

Spa Included

Every stay includes full access to our award-winning wellness sanctuary. Unwind completely.

The AirGlo Wellness Sanctuary spans over 1,200 square metres and is included in every room rate — no surcharges, no booking fees. Guests enjoy unrestricted access to the heated indoor pool, hydrotherapy circuit, steam room, sauna, and fully equipped fitness centre throughout their stay.

Our team of certified therapists offer a full treatment menu — from deep tissue massage and hot stone therapy to Georgian wine baths and bespoke facials. Complimentary 30-minute sessions are available daily for suite guests. The sanctuary is open from 06:00 to 22:00 with extended hours for in-room treatments.

  • 1,200m² wellness sanctuary, fully included
  • Heated pool, sauna, steam & hydrotherapy
  • Complimentary 30-min treatment for suite guests
  • Georgian wine baths & signature facials
