Our Kitchen

Culinary Excellence, Around the Clock

Our signature restaurant is helmed by an executive chef with over two decades of Michelin-starred experience across Paris, Tokyo, and New York. The menu celebrates Georgian produce — heritage-breed meats, sun-ripened vegetables, and aged cheeses from local artisan producers — reimagined through a modern European lens.

Whether you join us for a leisurely breakfast at dawn, a business lunch in our private dining room, or a late-night supper after the city has quieted, our kitchen operates with the same commitment to excellence at every hour.
